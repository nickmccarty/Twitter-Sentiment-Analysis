The Rationale Behind Our Neural Architecture

As shown below, Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs) take two inputs at each time step. Consequently, in order to capture semantic meanings in long sequences, we would need to run RNNs over many time steps, turning the unrolled RNN into a very deep network.
