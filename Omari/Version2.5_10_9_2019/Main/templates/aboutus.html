About Us

The who and why of SKOND Data

ERASE HATE

We are a group of data fellows, commited to fighting hate and intolerance in our communities.

We have built this service to allow users a helping hand in identifying hate speech. We use machine learning and neural networks as a way of programmaticaly classifying hate speech. Please keep in mind that the classifications we provide are predictions, and are not explicitly definitive. Please use discrection, consider all facts and intentions, before taking any actions based on the results provided. If you are interested in helping us achieve our mission please contact us!
